⚠️ BMW / Luxury Vehicle MMT Warning
High-performance boosters use MMT (metallic additive). In BMW N55, N54, B58, and similar engines, daily use can foul O2 sensors ($300–400 ea.) and clog catalytic converters ($1,500–2,500+). One sensor failure erases ~1.5 years of savings. Use only for emergencies or track days.
